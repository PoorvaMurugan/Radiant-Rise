About Us

Building Dreams, Creating Legacies

RadiantRise Lifespaces Private Limited is driven by a legacy of excellence in real estate. Founded and promoted by  Rajaraman Mathusuthanan, who brings more than two decades of experience covering every aspect of residential development, from land acquisition and project conceptualization to customer relationship management and end-to-end delivery.

With a portfolio spanning Chennai, Coimbatore, Thiruvanamalai, and Kumbakonam, RadiantRise has successfully completed landmark projects such as:

  • - The Riviera apartments (220 units, Chennai, 2006)
  • - Palm Meadows villas (200 units, Chennai, 2009)
  • - Premium villas (41 units sold out, Kovilancherry-Medavakkam, 2024)
  • - Large-scale plotted and villa developments across Tamil Nadu — over 800 units in Thiruvanamalai

Our Legacy

Rajaraman Mathusuthanan began his real estate journey with Casagrande, where he contributed as a shareholder and managed key verticals including layout planning, land acquisition, and customer relations. Landmark achievements include:

Leading developments under Casagrande across Chennai and nearby regions Delivering high-quality residential layouts and villa projects Developing over 100 acres in Thiruvanamalai and 12 acres in Coimbatore with diverse residential offerings RadiantRise Lifespaces continually expands its footprint, acquiring prime lands and executing successful projects that redefine community living.

Vision

To redefine community living by creating inspiring spaces that combine modern comfort, sustainable design, and lasting value. RadiantRise aspires to be the benchmark in delivering quality residences, forging vibrant neighborhoods that enrich lives and future generations.

Mission

To consistently deliver exceptional residential projects with uncompromising integrity, innovation, and customer satisfaction. Through thoughtful land acquisition, meticulous planning, and expert execution, RadiantRise is committed to transforming dreams into homes and crafting enduring relationships built on trust.
